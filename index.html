KIWI KIDS

Grade Pre K to Grade 4

Kiwi Kids Program (Pre-K to Grade 4) uses computerized tests to pinpoint strengths and weaknesses, then builds strengths while addressing gaps, following Utah Core Standards. Our engaging curriculum inspires creativity, builds confidence, and prepares eager children for future academic success.

  • Standards: Utah Core Standards
  • Approved By: Utah Education Fits All
  • Instructor: State Certified Teacher

Grade 5 to Grade 8

At Kiwi, students in Grades 5 through 8 focus on science. They explore exciting topics like physics, chemistry, and biology, taught by certified teachers and PhD-level instructors. Students visit labs at the University of Utah twice a month to see science in action.

  • Standards: Utah Core Standards
  • Approved By: Utah Education Fits All
  • Instructor: State Certified Teacher
  • Lab Visit: 2 / Month
  • Focus Area: Science

Special Needs Education

Kiwi's Special Needs program follows Utah Standards and is led by certified teachers with a minimum of 10 years of experience and para-educators. The program combines academic learning with sensory activities and research-based interactive equipment to promote neuroplasticity, helping students thrive academically and socially.

  • Batch Size: Maximum 3 on 1
  • Standards: Utah Core Standards
  • Approved By: Utah Education Fits All
  • Instructor: Special Needs State Certified Teacher
  • Focus Area: Academics, Behavior, Cognitive Activities
  • Research Approval: Harvard, NASA, UM
  • Location: Draper, South Jordan, Provo, Pleasant Grove

Kiwi Gym - Utah's Only Cognitive Fitness

Kiwi Gym’s Cognitive Fitness Program enhances brain performance through engaging physical and mental exercises. Tailored for all ages, it combines memory challenges, problem-solving tasks, and targeted workouts designed to boost concentration, reaction time, and overall mental agility.

  • Instructor: Certified Teacher
  • Visit: Weekly
